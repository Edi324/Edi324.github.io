Step 1

Take 125g of butter and let it melt in a bowl. Then stir it until peaks start to form
(for that, you lift up the object you're stirring with, and if little spikes stay up then you are done).

Step 2

Now you add 3 eggs, 125g sugar and a pinch of salt. After you added them in, you mix until the substance becomes a light color.
If there are tiny chunks of butter still in there, then its nothing to worry about.

Step 3

Now add half a tablespoon of vanilla sugar (in this instance i used vanillin sugar, but they are basically the same)
and 1 and a half dl of milk. Then mix everything.

Step 4

Add 250g flour and a tablespoon of baking powder. Then you stir until the substance becomes creamy.
You can also preheat the oven to 180Â° while you do that.

Step 5 (optional)

If you'd like, you can cut up a chocolate bar and add the pieces to your cake.
Chocolate chips are also fine of course.

Step 6

Now you can pour your mix into the cakeform, and leave it in the oven for around 25-30 minutes. Insert a toothpick or a knife to
check whether your cake is ready, if it is still wet, then leave it in for a little longer. If the knife is dry, then you can take the cake out.
*Please ignore how sad the cake looks in the after picture, I put too little baking powder here. It usually looks better I promise.

Step 7

Cut yourself a slice and enjoy it! I sure did.
